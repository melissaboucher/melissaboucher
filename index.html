Melissa Boucher
Product Designer

I'm interested in tools that help us to see, understand, and think.

I currently design productivity apps at a startup in the San Francisco Bay Area. I've studied Systems Engineering at the University of Pennsylvania and Industrial Engineering and Human Factors at San Jose State University.

My journey

Before joining an early-stage startup, I worked for several years at Zazzle, where I collaborated to build an e-commerce platform for custom goods. During graduate school, I partnered with a robotics lab to design and develop a Brain-Computer Interface application that would allow mobility-impaired persons to read on an Android device.

Prior to that, I was a medical student who, midway through long hours in the anatomy lab, realized she wanted to spend her days designing and building things.
