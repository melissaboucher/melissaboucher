Melissa Boucher
Product Designer

I'm interested in tools that help us to see, understand, and think.

I design software at startup companies in the San Francisco Bay Area. I've studied Systems Engineering at the University of Pennsylvania, Industrial Engineering and Human Factors at San Jose State University, and typography with the School of Visual Arts.

My journey

Before joining early-stage startups, I worked at Zazzle building an online marketplace for custom goods. During graduate school, I partnered with a robotics lab to design and develop a Brain-Computer-Interface application that would enable mobility-impaired persons to read on mobile devices.

Prior to that, I was a medical student who, midway through long hours in the anatomy lab, realized she wanted to spend her days designing and building things.
